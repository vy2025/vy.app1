Description

Introducing Sielify: a fresh, new way to explore and engage with conferences. Sielify is built with attendees and administrators in mind to ensure a seamless conference experience for all.

Feature highlights:
> AGENDA VIEW - browse conference events and add to personal agenda
> EVENTS VIEW - browse event schedules and get up-to-date info on prelim and final rounds
> ANNOUNCEMENTS - speedy communication between attendees and organizers
> CONFERENCE HOME - hub for external links and documents alongside schedule at-a-glance
